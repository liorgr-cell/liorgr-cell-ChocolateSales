## 📊 Project Overview & Data Source
This project performs an in-depth analysis of the global confectionery market. The foundational data used for this study was sourced from **Kaggle**.

The research simulates a 4-year market progression (2022-2025) to analyze how inflationary pressures, currency fluctuations, and varying price elasticities impact consumer behavior and corporate Total Revenue (TR).

## 🧪 Research Objectives & Methodology
1. **Data Cleaning & Normalization:** - Converting raw strings into standard ISO date formats.
   - Sanitizing currency symbols (USD) and casting fields to numeric types (REAL) for mathematical processing.
2. **Economic Simulation:** - Building SQL-based logic to simulate annual price increases and their impact on Quantity Demanded.
   - Differentiating between **Elastic** and **Inelastic** demand responses across different product lines (Milk, Dark, and Luxury chocolates).
3. **Revenue Analysis:** - Calculating and tracking **Total Revenue (TR)** trends to identify optimal pricing points under inflationary conditions.
